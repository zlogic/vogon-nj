Registration

To register, select the Register tab and enter a username and password.

Press Register and you'll be registered logged into your account.

Notice

If you have entered an already existing username, you'll see an error and should use another username and password.

Warning

Registration is simple, we don't ask your contact details or stupid questions like your favorite food :) And so there's no way of recovering passwords!

Warning

This may be a non-production deployment of Vogon and may be reset or closed by the site admin without notice. Consider exporting your data frequently or contacting the website administrator to confirm your data won't be destroyed without notice.

About Vogon

Vogon is an open-source personal finance tracker.

It runs on most cloud providers, even on free tiers.
